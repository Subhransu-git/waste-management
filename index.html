Recycling Tips

How to Recycle Right

Recycling is one of the simplest yet most effective ways to reduce waste and protect the environment. Here are some important recycling tips:

  • Sort Your Waste: Make sure to separate recyclables (paper, cardboard, plastic, metal) from non-recyclables (food waste, diapers, etc.).
  • Know What Can and Can’t Be Recycled:
  • Recyclable: Paper, plastic bottles, aluminum cans, glass bottles, cardboard.
  • Non-Recyclable: Styrofoam, plastic bags, food-soiled items, certain types of plastic
  • Clean and Dry: Rinse containers to remove food residue before recycling.
  • Check Local Guidelines: Recycling rules can vary by location, so always refer to your local waste management guidelines.
